Technically strong, practical in execution.

VDW Projects & Solutions supports electrical projects with a combination of technical knowledge, work preparation and project-based follow-up. Always with a focus on feasible solutions, clear communication and an execution that holds up.

A pragmatic approach to electrical projects

VDW Projects & Solutions was founded by Wouter Van De Wiele and supports both industrial and residential electrical projects. The focus is on a strong combination of technical expertise, clear structure and an approach that remains workable in practice.

In industrial environments, this often involves electrical infrastructure, medium voltage, energy distribution and the preparation and follow-up of complex projects. The emphasis is on engineering, work preparation, technical coordination and support during execution.

In residential projects, the focus shifts to safe, compliant and future-proof installations such as EV chargers, renovations and energy management. There too, a clean execution, logical design and clear communication remain central.

By combining technical knowledge with project-based follow-up, the result is an approach that is not only correct on paper, but that also works on site and during execution.
