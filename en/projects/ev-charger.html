Residential installation – Smart charge management (2025)

Project type: Private full installation

For this client, a smart electrical installation was provided, with attention to comfort, safety and efficiency.

🚗 EV charger with smart control

  • Individual EV charger with load-balancing configuration
  • The system automatically pauses when there isn't enough available power, so total consumption stays under the capacity tariff
  • If solar panels are present, the installation can be set to charge only on solar energy when available

📷 Smart video doorbell with indoor units

  • Video doorbell with touchscreen indoor units
  • Full smartphone integration for notifications, remote control and communication
